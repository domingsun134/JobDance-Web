# JobDance vs Fuku.ai - AI Interview Practice Benchmark

## Overview
This document compares JobDance AI Interview Practice platform with Fuku.ai to identify strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement.

## Feature Comparison

### 1. AI Conversation Quality

#### JobDance ✅
- **Context-Aware Responses**: AI references specific details from user's answers
- **Negative Response Handling**: Properly acknowledges when users say "I don't have experience"
- **Question Repetition Prevention**: Tracks previous questions and prevents repetition
- **Natural Voice**: Uses AWS Polly neural voices (Ruth) for human-like speech
- **Real-time Voice Conversation**: Full voice-to-voice interaction
- **Conversation Flow**: Automatically progresses through topics

#### Fuku.ai (Based on Research)
- **Reported Issues**: Users report AI repeating same questions
- **Limited Context**: May struggle with contextual understanding
- **Generic Responses**: Tends to give generic follow-ups

**Winner**: JobDance - Better context awareness and repetition prevention

---

### 2. Voice & Audio Features

#### JobDance ✅
- **Real-time AI Speech**: AWS Polly neural TTS (Ruth voice)
- **Voice Input**: Speech-to-text for user responses
- **Automatic Turn-taking**: AI speaks → User speaks → AI responds
- **Voice/Text Toggle**: Users can switch between voice and text modes
- **Natural Intonation**: Neural engine provides natural prosody

#### Fuku.ai
- **Voice Features**: Limited information available
- **Audio Quality**: Unknown
